This is a website that introduces a movie series called "Chinatown Detective" which includes 3 movies. This is a comedy suspense film from China, directed by Chen Sicheng and starring Wang Baoqiang and Liu Haoran. This movie is very interesting and loved by many people.

About the Director

Sicheng Chen was born on February 22, 1978 in Shenyang, Liaoning, China. He graduated from the Central Academy of Drama, and is known for his leading roles in the films A Young Prisoner's Revenge and Spring Fever; as well as television series Soldiers Sortie. He is an actor and writer, known for Tang ren jie tan an (2015), Bei Jing ai qing gu shi (2014) and Tang ren jie tan an 2 (2018).

Tang Ren


Tang Ren is a " detective" in Chinatown. He is not particularly smart but often provides Qin Feng with inspiration. Tang Ren likes that money and the beauty and he speak Mandarin with a Cantonese accent which make audience laugh a lot.He is played by Wang Baoqiang.



Qing Feng


Qin Feng is a talented young man with excellent memory and observation skills. His dream is to complete a perfect crime. He met his uncle Tang Ren and formed a Chinatown detective team because he did not get into the police academy.He is played by Liu Haoran.
